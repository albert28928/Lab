研究方向

Research

    Computer Vision

    Computer Vision is a field that enables computers to interpret and understand visual information from the world, such as images and videos. It combines computer science, mathematics, and artificial intelligence to allow machines to process and analyze visual data.

    Image Processing

    Image Processing is a technique used to enhance, manipulate, and analyze images. It involves applying various algorithms and operations to digital images to improve their quality or extract useful information.

    Federated Learning

    Federated Learning is a machine learning approach that allows multiple devices or servers to collaboratively train a shared model without sharing their raw data. Instead, each device trains the model locally and only shares updates with a central server.
