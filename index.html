研究方向

Research

    Computer Vision

    電腦視覺為本實驗室的核心研究領域，任何與電腦視覺相關之最新人工智慧技術，都在本實驗室研究範圍內，涵蓋物件偵測、圖像生成以及多模態技術整合等。


    Computer vision is the core focus of our lab, encompassing the latest AI technologies related to this field. Our research spans a wide range, from object detection and image generation to multimodal technology integration.

    Federated Learning

    透過聯邦式學習架構進行跨攝影機物件偵測，實現多來源領域自適應物件偵測，著重於領域差異性與資料類別不平衡之分析與深入研究。


    We employ a federated learning framework for cross-camera object detection, enabling adaptive detection across multiple sources while focusing on addressing domain discrepancies and data imbalance through thorough analysis and research.

    Technological Investigation

    將最新電腦視覺、深度學習、大數據分析等技術應用於真實案例。包含環境資料異常監測、環保犯罪偵查、跨攝影機可疑人車偵測等實際應用場景。


    We apply the latest technologies in computer vision, deep learning, and big data analysis to real-world cases, including environmental anomaly detection, environmental crime investigation, and cross-camera detection of suspicious vehicles and pedestrians.
